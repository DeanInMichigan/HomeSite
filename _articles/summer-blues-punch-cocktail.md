The Summer Blues Punch Cocktail is a great way to kick off an evening. This giant blue beverage is full of delicious flavor — make it in a large batch and share with a crowd.

## Ingredients

- 3½ oz (105ml) vodka
- 3½ oz (105ml) triple sec
- 2 oz (60ml) Blue Curaçao
- Lemonade, to top
- Strawberries, for garnish
- Lemon slices, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Combine the vodka, triple sec, and Blue Curaçao in a large pitcher or punch bowl.
2. Top with lemonade to taste.
3. Garnish with strawberries and lemon slices. Serve over ice.
