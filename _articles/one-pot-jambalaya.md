## Ingredients

- Olive oil
- Cajun seasoning
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- Bell peppers, chopped
- 2 chicken breasts
- Andouille sausage
- Shrimp
- Black beans
- Rice
- Tomatoes
- Chicken stock

## Instructions

1. Coat chicken with oil and Cajun seasoning and quickly sear.
2. Add chopped onion and diced peppers and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add sausage, shrimp, black beans, rice, tomatoes, chicken stock, and Cajun seasoning. Simmer for 25 minutes.
