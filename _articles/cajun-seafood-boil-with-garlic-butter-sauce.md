This is the BEST Seafood Boil recipe you can whip up right at home. Loaded with jumbo shrimp, crab, andouille sausage, potatoes, eggs, and corn simmered in a Cajun broth, then smothered in an epic garlic butter sauce. Serves 6.

## Ingredients

**Cajun seafood boil:**

- 3 quarts water
- 1 (12-ounce) can of beer (optional but recommended)
- 3 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
- 1 tablespoon Old Bay seasoning
- Hot sauce, to taste
- 1 medium yellow onion, roughly sliced into half-moons
- 1 large lemon, cut into wedges (plus more for serving)
- 12 ounces andouille sausage, sliced into rounds
- 1 lb baby potatoes, red or gold
- 1 lb pre-cooked snow crab leg clusters, fresh or frozen
- 1–1½ lbs jumbo shrimp, deveined
- 4–6 ears sweet corn on the cob
- 4–6 hard boiled eggs (optional)

**Garlic butter sauce:**

- 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
- 10 cloves garlic, finely minced
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice, freshly squeezed
- 1 tablespoon Old Bay seasoning
- 1 tablespoon fresh chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- Hot sauce, to taste

## Instructions

1. In an extra-large stockpot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat, combine the water and beer (if using). Bring to a boil. Season with Cajun seasoning, Old Bay, and a few dashes of hot sauce. Add the sliced onion and lemon wedges. Let boil for 15 minutes.
2. Add the andouille sausage and baby potatoes. Cook for 15–20 minutes, until the potatoes are just about fork-tender.
3. Gently add the crab clusters, shrimp, and corn. Make sure everything is submerged and stir to combine. Boil for another 5–7 minutes until the shrimp is pink. Meanwhile, prepare the garlic butter sauce.
4. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the butter, garlic, lemon juice, Old Bay, parsley, Cajun seasoning, smoked paprika, and hot sauce. Stir and simmer until butter melts and sauce is well combined, about 5–7 minutes. Remove from heat.
5. Line an extra-large baking sheet with foil. Use a spider strainer to remove the boil contents from the pot onto the sheet (discard the onion and lemon bits). Add the hard boiled eggs if using.
6. Pour the garlic butter sauce over everything. Toss with your hands to smother every piece in sauce. Serve immediately with extra lemon wedges.
