## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 2 cups heavy cream
- ½ cup Swerve confectioners sweetener
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 teaspoons maple extract
- 1 tablespoon ChocZero Maple Pecan syrup (or sweetener of choice)
- 1 tablespoon MCT oil
- 2 tablespoons pecans, toasted and chopped

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter, heavy cream, Swerve sweetener, and salt in a small saucepan over low heat. Do not boil.
2. Whisk the egg yolks until light in color. Temper them by stirring in a spoonful of the warm butter cream mixture, then continue with a few more spoonfuls. Gradually stir the yolks into the saucepan.
3. Continue stirring over low heat until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon (175°F). Pour into a bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes to cool.
4. Stir in the maple extract, ChocZero syrup, and MCT oil.
5. Pour into your ice cream machine and follow the manufacturer's instructions. Stir in the pecans, then spread into an 8×5-inch loaf pan. Freeze for 2–3 hours for soft serve consistency.

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

302 kcal · Serves 8 at ½ cup each
