## Ingredients

- ¼–½ cup low sodium soy sauce
- 1 medium onion
- 12 sprigs fresh thyme
- 6 scallions/green onions
- 8 garlic cloves
- 2 scotch bonnet or habanero peppers
- A knuckle of fresh ginger
- ½ teaspoon ground cloves
- ¼ cup ground allspice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1¼ cups freshly squeezed lemon or lime juice
- ¼ cup canola, vegetable, or olive oil

## Instructions

1. In a blender, add the soy sauce, onion, thyme, scallions, garlic, peppers, and ginger. Pulse 2–3 times then blend briefly.
2. Add the ground cloves, allspice, salt, sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, black pepper, and lemon juice. Blend well.
3. Drizzle in the oil and blend until you have a smooth purée. Taste and adjust seasonings.

## Notes

- If using a food processor, add all dry ingredients first, blend until smooth, then add liquids.
- Use fewer hot peppers if you prefer a milder sauce.
