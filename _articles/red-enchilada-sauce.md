## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 2 cups water
- 3 oz tomato paste
- ½ teaspoon cumin
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ¾ teaspoon salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Add the vegetable oil, flour, and chili powder to a medium pot. Turn heat to medium and whisk together. Continue whisking as the mixture begins to bubble. Cook for one minute once bubbling.
2. Whisk in the water, tomato paste, cumin, garlic powder, and cayenne until smooth. Allow to come to a simmer — it will begin to thicken slightly.
3. Add salt to taste, starting with ½ teaspoon. The sauce is ready to use.
