## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground pork
- 1 (4-ounce) can green chiles, with juice
- 1 jalapeño
- ⅔ cup white onion
- 10 tablespoons La Victoria green chili salsa
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons cumin
- 3¼ cups water
- 1 (1¼-ounce) package Ortega taco seasoning (hot and spicy)

## Instructions

1. Brown ground pork and onions together in a large pot.
2. Add diced chiles and La Victoria salsa. Stir to combine.
3. Add 3 cups of water and the Ortega taco seasoning.
4. Add salt and cumin.
5. Mix the cornstarch with the remaining ¼ cup water, then stir into the sauce.
6. Continue to simmer until thickened.
