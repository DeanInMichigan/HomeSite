Prep Time: 5 minutes · Cook Time: 25 minutes · Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cooking oil
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced into bite-sized strips
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 1 large carrot, sliced
- ½ small red bell pepper, sliced
- ½ small yellow bell pepper, sliced
- ½ small orange bell pepper, sliced
- 2 cups water
- ½ (6-ounce) can of tomato paste
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1 teaspoon chili powder or fresh sliced red chili (optional)
- Parsley, for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken, season with salt and black pepper, and sear for about 2 minutes on each side until golden.
2. Add the onion, carrot, and bell peppers. Season with salt and cook for 5–7 minutes until crisp-tender.
3. Stir in the water, tomato paste, garlic, cumin, paprika, and optional chili. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until the sauce thickens and the chicken reaches 165°F internal temperature.
4. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley, alongside rice or pasta.
