## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup vegetable oil (you can substitute applesauce for half the oil)
- 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Grease two 8×4-inch bread pans or 6 mini loaf pans. Preheat oven to 325°F.
2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
3. Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar together in a large bowl.
4. Add the dry ingredients to the egg mixture and stir until combined.
5. Grate the zucchini. Stir into the mixture along with the nuts until well combined. Pour batter into prepared pans.
6. Bake for 40–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Mini loaf pans take about 35–40 minutes; large loaves take about 55 minutes.
7. Cool in pan on a rack for 20 minutes, then remove and cool completely.
