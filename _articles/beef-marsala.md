## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 finely diced shallots, or 1 white onion, finely diced
- 1½ pounds mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
- 2 cups Marsala wine
- 1 cup beef stock
- 1 tablespoon freshly chopped thyme leaves
- 8 (8 to 10-ounce) New York Strip steaks
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- ½ cup unsalted butter (1 stick)
- ½ bunch fresh chives, chopped

## Instructions

1. Begin the Marsala sauce by heating the olive oil in a large sauté pan. Cook the shallots (or onion) and mushrooms gently until translucent. Deglaze with the wine, add the stock and fresh thyme, and allow to reduce by two-thirds.
2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat and season meat on both sides with salt and pepper. Sear on both sides and grill to about 128°F internal temperature for medium-rare. Remove from grill and let rest.
3. Strain the Marsala sauce and whisk in butter to finish. Season with salt and pepper as needed. Spoon the sauce over the grilled steaks, garnish with chopped chives, and serve.
