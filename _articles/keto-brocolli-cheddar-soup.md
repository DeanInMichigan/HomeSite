## Ingredients

- 1 cup broccoli
- 5 oz cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 ounces cream cheese
- ¼ cup onion
- ¼ cup celery
- 1½ cups chicken broth
- ½ cup heavy whipping cream
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Cut broccoli into pieces and set aside.
2. Add butter, onion, celery, salt, and pepper to a saucepan. Cook until onions and celery are translucent.
3. Add broccoli and cook for an additional 2–3 minutes until broccoli turns bright green.
4. Add chicken broth and heavy whipping cream. Bring to a boil.
5. Reduce to a simmer and slowly add the cheddar cheese, stirring as you go.
6. Once fully incorporated, add the cream cheese and stir until the soup thickens. Serve warm.
