Prep: 20 min · Cook: 2 hours · Serves: 5

## Ingredients

- 30 oz 80% lean ground beef
- 5 slices bacon, finely chopped
- ¾ medium white onion
- 1 small green bell pepper
- 1 medium roma tomato
- 2 medium jalapeño peppers
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon pink salt
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cups chicken or beef stock

## Instructions

1. Add the finely chopped bacon to a large pot or Dutch oven. Cook down until the fat is rendered and the bacon is nearly done.
2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
3. Finely chop the garlic and jalapeños. Rough chop the tomato, bell pepper, and onion.
4. Add all remaining ingredients along with the stock. Simmer for 1–3 hours — the longer it cooks, the better it tastes.
5. Near the end of cooking, remove the lid and cook uncovered until most of the liquid has evaporated. Enjoy.
